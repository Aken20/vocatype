# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in VocaType a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged:**
- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Giving credit where it's due
- Focusing on what's best for the project
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

**Not tolerated:**
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team via GitHub issues. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
